About

Del Frisco's Grille is located in the heart of Mid-Town at Rockefeller Center. A bit more casual than Del Frisco's Double Eagle Steakhouse, The Grille offers Hand Cut Prime Steaks, Fire Roasted Flat-breads, Hearty Salads, Fresh Seafood, and much more.... We offer a 700 bottle selection wine list, that offers a taste for every palate. Join us on our outdoor patio on the historic Rockefeller Plaza, or inside at the bar or one of our comfortable tables and experience our signature hospitality. Outdoor seating available on a first come first serve basis.
